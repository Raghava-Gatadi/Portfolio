Research Work

My research explores the intersection of cybersecurity, deep learning, and hardware security. I aim to design intelligent and efficient systems capable of resisting evolving cyber threats.

Federated Learning for Intrusion Detection

Investigated federated learning approaches to secure IoT networks by decentralizing intrusion detection. Designed and tested CNN and Autoencoder-based models to detect malicious traffic across distributed nodes.

PUF-Based Hardware Security & Cryptanalysis

Conducted cryptanalysis on multiple PUF-based hardware authentication schemes. Proposed a hybrid model improving key-generation consistency and physical resistance under environmental variations.

Deep Learning for Fake News Detection

Researched NLP-driven detection systems using BERT and Word2Vec embeddings for misinformation classification. Enhanced text representation to achieve a 30% accuracy boost on benchmark datasets.
